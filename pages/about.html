Founder

Sk

Est.2019

Sk is a creative collective started in New York City and Durban, South Africa. It is run by myself and a team of amazing creators from South Africa (Amy Aurther, Tristan Stellenburg, Wazana Moncho, Sanikwa and Kutloano). Our mission is to create a community that celebrates self-expression and creativity, through the distribution and creation of content.

Sk was started in April 2019, our first goal was to connect and scout like-minded creators to collaborate and work with. We originally started with 30 creators from all around the world. We then planned and organized a release campaign that air in November 2019. Since then Sk has successfully managed multiple projects and is steadily growing in reach. You can find out more about some of my contributions as a Project manager in the lower section.

We aim to provide our followers with artistic and inspirational content while also creating a platform for creators to gain exposure and connect with other creators from countries such as the United States, the United Kingdom, South Africa, and Spain. Our target market is a youth with an interest in art fashion and music. The advantage Sk has is that as new content is released on the platform it leads to greater exposure and potential followers. This will lead to more creators gaining interest in the page allowing for more unique content and collaborations

We plan to expand our platform to more social media applications such as YouTube and Spotify as well as develop a brand website for articles, media content like playlist, videos, and e-commerce.
